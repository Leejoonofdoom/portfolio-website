    About me

    I’ve been a part of many amazing design teams developing for a variety of industries. Currently, I am designing experiences at Shutterstock for their two-sided ecommerce and contributor marketplace. Previously, I developed enterprise experiences for advisors and investors at J.P. Morgan Chase & Co. and I consulted with the Coach USA bus company helping them build a digital strategy for web and mobile.

    Aside from work, I am a student of filmmaking and archery. I seek out new places to eat and travel.

    I’m currently based in the Greater New York area.
