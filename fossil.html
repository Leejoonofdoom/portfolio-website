    Fossil

    ×

    My role

    Design lead

    Duration

    1.5 years

    Activities

    UX/UI design, user research

    Platform

    Mobile app

    Overview

    In 2015, Fossil Group noticed a decline in traditional watch sales and saw an opportunity to shift in another direction and integrate smart functionality into their watches called the Hybrid Smartwatch.

    In 2016, the wearables market was evolving. So, with an inherited designfrom a creative agency and a newly formed design team at the start of 2017, Fossil had the opportunity to redesign the app to best meet the user’s needs and provide greater functionality.

    The design team was tasked to redefine the Hybrid watch experience.

    Challenges

    Our goal was to evaluate the architecture of the existing app and restructure to support new technology and build scalable customization across brand portfolio. 2 key new features were adding a Home screen and Shortcuts.

    Our ambition was to create a firm foundation for more brands to follow that would give the user a wholesome experience.

    Our high level goals were to:

    1. Simplify and create an intuitive experience
    2. Drive engagement to the brands and inside the app
    3. Unlock new features

    My role

    I was hired specifically to work on redesigning the Fossil Q App that pairs with the hybrid smartwatch. Working side-by-side with my UX partner, we designed the user experience for the entire app. After developing initial concepts, l focused mainly on the experience of assigning notifications from contacts and apps to the watch.

    The team consisted of 3 designers heads down working on the app for the length of the project. In addition, I worked alongside 2 Product managers, a Content Strategist and developers.
    As the project moved to UI design, I worked out UX Documentation and animations for development.

    The app launched globally on October 22nd, 2017.

    CUSTOMER INSIGHTS

    I partnered with the Customer Care team to gain insight into our customer's pain points and feedback.

    CONCEPT CREATION

    I wireframed the core features of the app with another UX designer before splitting off and creating them digitally.

    PROTOTYPING

    I created prototypes and user interaction demonstrations for stakeholders and development and to use for user testing.

    USERTESTING

    Prior to me joining, there wasn't an established user research practice. So, I spearheaded the user testing process of finding users, creating the tests, conducting the tests and analysis.

    UX DOCUMENTATION

    I created a UX specifications document consisting of component definitions, spacing, and user flows during the project to communicate requirements to the engineering team.

    ANIMATIONS

    I created animations of user interactions for the engineering team to supplement the UX specifications.

    RESULTS

    FOSSIL Q APP REDESIGN LAUNCH

    We successfully launched the redesigned app on schedule. It was an amazing success considering the scale and complexity of the project and completing it in 8 months.
