    Prudential Financial

    ×

    My role

    Design lead

    Duration

    6 months

    Activities

    UX/UI design, user research

    Platform

    Mobile app

    Overview

    In 2019, Prudential realized the need to bring a richer mobile experience for their retirement product offerings as an upgrade to their outdated desktop web experience.

    Prudential initially launched their mobile app with Contributions as research showed contributing to a 401k was a major activity for users. The next goal was to add more features and improve financial wellness by adding Investment Allocations and Transfers.

    DESIGN SPRINT

    At Prudential, we started initiatives with design sprints to validate our ideas and assumptions. We held a few working sessions and gathered customer research before the sprint week.

    We concluded the sprint with a working prototype and specific next steps. We held further working sessions to build out the rest of the experience.

    How might we streamline updating various investment activities and ensure safety and privacy?

    EXECUTION MODE

    With the happy paths mapped out, we still needed to create all of the edge cases and nuances that we couldn't fit into the design sprint. It took many months of iterating, vetting the designs with the retirement team, legal team and compliance.

    The biggest and most complex challenge was figuring out how to include all the mandatory messages we needed to show the users as they allocated to different funds and transferred funds between accounts. This required the team to develop several iterations on a messaging system.

    INVESTMENT ALLOCATIONS KEY INTERACTIONS

    Investments tab

    Investment options

    Adjust or add or remove allocations

    Review changes before confirming

    Confirmation of changes

    TRANSFERS KEY INTERACTIONS

    Investments tab

    Investment options

    Select which fund to transfer from

    Make changes to equal 100%

    Confirmation of changes

    To make a transfer, the user would select the from fund and one or more to funds and adjust the percentages. They would be able to review their changes before submitting and then receive a confirmation.

    APP LAUNCH

    We successfully launched both features: Investment Allocations in November 2019 and Transfers in January 2020. With managing such complexity and many moving parts, it was incredible we were able to successfully launch and provide much desired functionality to our customer base on the go.
