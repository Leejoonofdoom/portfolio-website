    Duration

    Activities/deliverables

    UX/UI design, user research

    Platform

    Overview

    During my time at Shutterstock, a global leader in stock content, I contributed to enhancing both sides of its dynamic two-sided marketplace:

    1. Customer Experience (E-commerce): I focused on improving the search and discovery experience to help customers efficiently find the perfect content for their creative projects. My efforts included optimizing site navigation and refining search functionality, ensuring a seamless journey from exploration to purchase.

    2. Contributor Experience (Artists and Photographers): On the contributor side, I worked to reduce friction in the submission process and drive greater engagement. By streamlining and incorporating education throughout the workflows, I empowered artists and photographers to contribute high-quality, desirable assets to our growing library.

    This dual-impact role highlights my ability to enhance both user experience and operational efficiency in a complex, multi-faceted ecosystem.
