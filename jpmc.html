    Design lead

    Duration

    2 years

    Activities

    UX/UI design, user research

    Platform

    Internal wealth platform

    Overview

    Connect is an enterprise desktop Wealth Management platform serving over 20,000 users from Front Office Advisors, Client Services to Operations and more. The Connect ecosystem consists of a series of applications (apps) that the product and design team manages the addition of new apps and redesigning or adding features to existing apps. Financial advisors and other users use Connect to onboard new clients, perform account maintenance and increase their client's net worth.

    My role

    I was a Senior UX Designer on the Connect Core Team. We managed the core aspects of the ecosystem that all users engaged with from our Connect Design System to our governance process to UX Consultancy.

    Below is a list of some of my key responsibilities:

    CONNECT DESIGN SYSTEM

    A list of my contributions:

    • Learned the ins and outs of creating and maintaining a widely used design system
    • I authored design documentation on numerous components, their best practices and the different variations that can be incorporated in a design
    • I proactively assessed and submitted enhancement requests to features that needed improvements

    UX GOVERNANCE

    The way the Connect platform is structured, different teams across the firm can add new features or apps into the ecosystem but they need to follow our governance process to ensure core principles are met for a consistent experience.

    When teams make it to the UX evaluation step of the process, my role was to meet with these teams to understand their business goals, the purpose of their application and evaluate the design to ensure it meets our design and ADA standards.

    UX Governance process

    UX CONSULTANCY

    Some teams that need new applications don't always have the UX design resources to help build their products. So depending on their needs, we provide either a full UX design process engagement or a limited engagement.

    I was the design lead on several projects simultaneously with each one in different stages of the design process. Below are a few notable projects that I am proud of:

    Extraction & Digitized Document Integration (EDDI)

    A document scanning application that greatly reduces manual data entry by digitizing documents and extracts data for various purposes. I designed the interface to conveniently identify and repair any extraction errors.

    Financial Overview and Financial Entry

    A financial modeling tool aggregating a clients internal and external accounts into one net worth view with the ability to easily include other assets and liabilities. I designed the dashboard view of a client's net worth that includes external accounts and a streamlined process to quickly add additional external entries into the view.

    Life Insurance

    A process that integrated brand new digital solutions into the advisor's workflow to add life insurance to their client's financial portfolio. I developed a strategy and experience of weaving between JPMC and 3rd party apps in a streamlined way where backend data flows back and forth which hasn't been achieved before.

    Project highlight

    ADVISOR & SERVICE PLATFORM END TO END RESEARCH PROJECT

    I've yet to see a group approach product development the right way in first understanding the root issues and user's needs instead of jumping right into building aimlessly until now. This opportunity was birthed from an executive not wanting to repeat the same mistakes year after year of poorly building subpar products and constantly redesigning them. He wanted to fix the current products and create a plan to prevent rework moving forward.

    The problem stemmed from a lack of unified decision making that resulted in a completely incoherent experience with apps that don't talk to each other not maintaining client context and unintuitive processes that places too much responsibility upon users's memories. This platform spans 4 lines of business, over 15 products, and 10 tech stacks.

    The goal was to create a truly consistent and intuitive platform experience. We started with a discovery research effort that spanned 6 months.

    My role was partnering with my UX researcher on putting the research plan (shown below) into action. I was the strategist taking the research findings data and shaping it into digestible stories. I also created future explorations designs for the ecosystem based on research.

    RESEARCH PLAN

    STAKEHOLDER PRESENTATION

    We presented our research findings to our senior leadership team which was well-received. We aligned on a path forward for next steps in socializing further with the rest of the impacted product teams and individually working with them on identifying the areas of greatest opportunities.
